Discover

Explore Dimapur

Your base camp for discovering the wonders of Nagaland — rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes await.

🏛️

Kachari Ruins

Ancient mushroom-shaped monoliths from the medieval Kachari Kingdom — a must-visit archaeological wonder.

📍 1 km away
🏰

Rajbari Ruins

Historical remains of the Kachari royal palace, offering a glimpse into Nagaland's rich pre-colonial heritage.

📍 2 km away
🔬

Nagaland Science Centre

Interactive science exhibits and a planetarium — perfect for families and curious travellers.

📍 3 km away
⛪

Ao Baptist Church

One of Dimapur's most prominent churches, known for its beautiful architecture and serene grounds.

📍 3 km away
🛍️

Hong Kong Market

Dimapur's famous shopping hub — electronics, clothing, and local handicrafts at bargain prices.

📍 1.5 km away
🎭

Hornbill Festival

The "Festival of Festivals" in December — Nagaland's grandest cultural celebration, just 74km at Kisama.

📍 74 km (Kohima)
